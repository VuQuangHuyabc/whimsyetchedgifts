Returns & Refunds Policy

Last updated: March 2024

30-Day Return Policy

You are entitled to cancel your order within 30 days without giving any reason for doing so.

The deadline for canceling an order is 30 days from the date you received goods or on which a third party you have appointed, who is not the carrier, takes possession of the product delivered.

In order to exercise your right of cancellation, you must inform us of your decision by means of a clear statement.

You can inform us of your decision by e-mail info@whimsyetchedgifts.com

We will reimburse you no later than 30 days from the day on which we receive the returned goods. We will use the same means of payment as you used for the order, and you will not incur any fees for such reimbursement.

Conditions for Returns

In order for the goods to be eligible for a return, please make sure that:

  • The goods were purchased in the last 30 days
  • The goods are in the original packaging

Non-Returnable Items

The following goods cannot be returned:

  • The supply of goods made to your specifications or clearly personalized.
  • The supply of goods which according to their nature are not suitable to be returned, for example goods which deteriorate rapidly or where the date of expiry is over.
  • The supply of goods which are not suitable for return due to health protection or hygiene reasons and were unsealed after delivery.
  • The supply of goods which are, after delivery, according to their nature, inseparably mixed with other items.

We reserve the right to refuse returns of any merchandise that does not meet the above return conditions at our sole discretion.

Returning Goods

You are responsible for the cost and risk of returning goods to us.

We cannot be held responsible for goods damaged or lost in return shipment. Therefore, we recommend an insured and trackable mail service. We are unable to issue a refund without actual receipt of goods or proof of received return delivery.

If the goods weren't marked as a gift when purchased, or the gift giver had the order shipped to themselves to give it to you later, we will send the refund to the gift giver.

Return Process

Step 1: Contact Us

Email us at info@whimsyetchedgifts.com with your order number and reason for return.

Step 2: Receive Return Authorization

We will review your request and provide a return authorization number if approved.

Step 3: Ship the Item

Package the item securely in original packaging and ship to the address we provide.

Step 4: Receive Refund

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within 30 days.

Refund Timeline

Standard Return 5-7 business days after receipt Original payment method
Defective Item 2-3 business days after receipt Original payment method + return shipping credit

6. Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item:

  • We will inspect the item within 3-5 business days
  • If approved, your refund will be processed
  • Refunds will be issued to your original payment method
  • Refunds typically appear within 5-10 business days
  • You will receive email confirmation when your refund is processed

7. Exchanges

We offer exchanges for items of equal or lesser value. If you choose an item of higher value, you will need to pay the difference.

Exchange items are subject to availability. If your preferred item is out of stock, we will process a refund instead.

8. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage and packaging
  • We will arrange for a replacement or full refund
  • No return shipping required for damaged items

9. Store Credit

As an alternative to refunds, we offer store credit that:

  • Never expires
  • Can be used on any future purchase
  • Includes a 10% bonus for future orders

10. Holiday Return Policy

For purchases made between November 15th and December 31st, we extend our return window to January 31st of the following year.

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions about our return policy, please contact us:

  • Email: returns@whimsyetchedgifts.com
  • Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567
  • Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM EST
Important Notice

This policy applies to all purchases made through our website. In-store purchases may have different return policies. Please retain your order confirmation and packaging until you are satisfied with your purchase.
