Returns & Refunds Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from Whimsy Etched Gifts. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free return policy.

30-Day Return Window: You may return items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

2. Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, items must:

  • Be unused and in the same condition as received
  • Be in the original packaging
  • Include all tags and accessories
  • Not be damaged or altered
  • Have proof of purchase (order confirmation or receipt)

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Custom or personalized items
  • Items marked as final sale
  • Items damaged due to customer misuse
  • Items returned after 30 days from delivery

4. How to Initiate a Return

To start the return process:

  1. Contact our customer service at returns@whimsyetchedgifts.com
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Wait for return authorization and shipping instructions
  4. Package the item securely in original packaging
  5. Attach the provided return shipping label
  6. Drop off the package at the specified carrier

5. Return Shipping

Free Return Shipping: We provide free return shipping for defective items or errors on our part.

Customer-Paid Returns: For other returns, customers are responsible for return shipping costs. Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

6. Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item:

  • We will inspect the item within 3-5 business days
  • If approved, your refund will be processed
  • Refunds will be issued to your original payment method
  • Refunds typically appear within 5-10 business days
  • You will receive email confirmation when your refund is processed

7. Exchanges

We offer exchanges for items of equal or lesser value. If you choose an item of higher value, you will need to pay the difference.

Exchange items are subject to availability. If your preferred item is out of stock, we will process a refund instead.

8. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage and packaging
  • We will arrange for a replacement or full refund
  • No return shipping required for damaged items

9. Store Credit

As an alternative to refunds, we offer store credit that:

  • Never expires
  • Can be used on any future purchase
  • Includes a 10% bonus for future orders

10. Holiday Return Policy

For purchases made between November 15th and December 31st, we extend our return window to January 31st of the following year.

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions about our return policy, please contact us:

  • Email: returns@whimsyetchedgifts.com
  • Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567
  • Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM EST
Important Notice

This policy applies to all purchases made through our website. In-store purchases may have different return policies. Please retain your order confirmation and packaging until you are satisfied with your purchase.
