Shipping Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-3 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays.

If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery.

2. Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Price
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days $5.00
Express Shipping 2-3 business days $15.00
Overnight Shipping 1 business day $25.00
Free Shipping 5-7 business days FREE (on orders over $50)

3. International Shipping

We currently ship within the United States only. International shipping options may be available in the future.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package using the tracking number on our website or the carrier's website.

5. Shipping Address

Please ensure that your shipping address is correct when placing your order. We are not responsible for packages delivered to incorrect addresses provided by the customer.

If you need to change your shipping address after placing an order, please contact us within 24 hours of placing your order.

6. Lost or Stolen Packages

We are not responsible for lost or stolen packages after they have been delivered to the shipping address. If you believe your package has been lost or stolen, please contact the shipping carrier directly.

7. Damaged Items

If your order arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damaged items and packaging. We will work with you to resolve the issue promptly.

8. Shipping Delays

We are not responsible for shipping delays caused by weather, natural disasters, or other circumstances beyond our control.

9. Customs and Duties

For international orders (when available), customers are responsible for any customs fees, import duties, or taxes charged by their country.
