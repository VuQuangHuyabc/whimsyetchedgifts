Shipping Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Shipping Information

Enjoy fast shipping with a 1-day preparation time and delivery in 2–3 days.

Shipping Policy FAQs

What is preparation time?

Our preparation time for orders is 1 day.

What is shipping time?

Shipping typically takes 2–3 days after preparation.

Do you offer expedited shipping?

Currently, we do not offer expedited shipping options. Standard shipping is 2–3 days.

How can I track my order?

You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has shipped.

What shipping carriers do you use?

We use reliable carriers to ensure timely delivery of your orders.

Can I change my shipping address?

Please contact us immediately if you need to change your shipping address before your order is shipped.

International Shipping

We currently ship within the United States only. International shipping options may be available in the future. Please contact us at info@whimsyetchedgifts.com for more information.

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1 business day. You will receive an email confirmation once your order has been placed and another email with tracking information once your order has shipped.

Lost or Damaged Items

If your order is lost or arrives damaged, please contact us immediately at info@whimsyetchedgifts.com. We will work with our shipping partners to resolve the issue quickly.
